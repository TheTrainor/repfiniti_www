How It Works

We all have talent. MNTR is designed to eliminate hassle and allow anyone to mentor in a subject of their choice.

Anyone who wants to teach can put their lesson and availability online.

Interested students find your lesson and book an available slot.

Our scheduling and payment system handles the rest. Just show up!

After the lesson, everyone leaves feedback in the app.
