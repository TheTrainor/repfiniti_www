By accessing any part of the REPFINITI Platform, you accept and agree to the terms and conditions of the REPFINITI Platforms Terms of Service ("Terms of Service"), which includes your consent to, and authorization of, the collection, use and disclosure of your personal information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. 1. Collection and Use of Your Personal Information

To provide functionality required by the REPFINITI Platform, you are required to provide certain personal information. A basic Member account is required to provide, but is not limited to, your email address and a password. You may, at your leisure, provide further information including your age, gender, height, weight and more, for the purpose of improving Repfiniti's CoachAI recommendations for you.

Throughout the course of using the REPFINITI Platform, your workout data may be processed along with any other optional metrics you provide for the purpose of training a Neural Network (NN) and providing you with tailored workouts. The goal of the NN is to analyze workout data, in aggregate, across all users of Repfiniti so as to be able to generate workouts tailored towards people with similar backgrounds and fitness ability.

Sunfall collects this data from you to facilitate the following functionality from the REPFINITI Platform:

  • Account creation, identification and authentication
  • Processing of Subscription requests
  • To provide and improve our services
  • To contact you
  • On an aggregate basis to help us understand our customers and grow our userbase
  • To provide or arrange for external payment services
  • To provide personalized workouts through CoachAI functionality
  • Training a neural network to provide optimal workouts for Subscribers
  • To provide online backup and sync ability for member workout data

REPFINITI utilizes 'Google Firebase' to collect statistics on app usage. These statistics are used by SUNFALL to see what features of the app are the most popular and get a feel for what our users like, which countries Repfiniti is most popular in, and various other things.

Sunfall may note and store details of your hardware setup, including but not limited to web browser, operating system, device, and any other software or hardware attributes deemed necessary to improve the REPFINITI Platform.

Although Sunfall is an Australian business, our data centres can be located in multiple locations across the Americas, Europe and Asia.

Your contact information may also be used for accounting, invoicing and billing purposes, marketing purposes, by third party service providers to Sunfall, and to respond to any enquiry you make, in some cases information we collect is transferred overseas. When you contact Sunfall, We may keep a record of the communication(s) between you and Sunfall to help resolve any issues you might have.

4. Protecting your Personal Information

All information sent between the REPFINITI Platform client apps and web servers is encrypted with TLS. Your account is protected by a password of your own choosing. Only the hashed (encrypted) form of your password is stored on our servers. We take all reasonable steps to ensure protection of your data, but cannot guarantee absolute security of our systems, or that we will not be the target of malicious hackers or viruses. In light of this, we cannot and do not ensure or warrant the security or privacy of your personal information. You transmit your personal details to us at your own risk.

You are encouraged to use strong passwords that are unique to the REPFINITI Platform (not shared with any other service you may use). If you have trouble remembering or creating strong passwords, there are a variety of secure, third party password managers available to assist you, including LastPass, KeePass, 1Password and Dashlane.
